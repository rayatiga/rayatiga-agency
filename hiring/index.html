Google Ads Copywriter

Open Temporary Position - Remote Work

Job Description

The ideal candidate is someone who has a passion for copywriting skills, and has experience in advertising products, especially digital products on the Google Ads platform (written in english).

Responsibilities

  • Compose text for advertising needs in a concise and clear manner
  • Operate Google Ads and determine the right budgeting for advertising
  • Operate Google Ads as an advertising ecosystem to place ad-ready in-house copywriting
  • Collaborate with the marketing to distribute digital products in the form of advertising promotional media
  • Compile a presentation file with Google Slides that includes agency and product information presented

Qualifications

  • Experience using Google Ads for at least 1 year
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Highly organized with excellent attention to detail
  • Having a certification such as Google Ads or similar is a big plus
  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience in advertising and copywriting
  • Have a portfolio of copywriting results especially advertising with Google Ads
